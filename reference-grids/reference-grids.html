Mountain-ranges masks#

The mountain ranges masks (0.5Â°, 1Â°, and 2Â°) have been defined using the K1 global mountains GIS datalayer ([Rmgsc.Cr.Usgs.Gov - /Outgoing/Ecosystems/Global/]; file: GlobalMountainsK1Binary.zip; Kapos et al. 2000). The 2Â° and 1Â° masks are upscaled versions of the 0.5Â° grid considering a 0.5 threshold for mountain area extent within the gridbox to better match the mountain areas in the different resolution grids.
